Your All Access Guide to the Beauty of the 944

The 944 was a front-engined sports car manufactured by Porsche between 1982 and 1991. It was rear wheel drive, and models either contained naturally aspirated or turbo engines. The 944 was a revolution in auto design due to its perfect 50/50 weight distribtion, with its engine in the front and the transmission in the rear. Its use in movies such as Sixteen Candles and its lower priced entry to the world of Porsche led to it quickly becoming one of the most recognizable and beloved cars of the 80's.
